Creamy Garlic Pasta

Ingredients

IngredientQuantity
Pasta200g
Garlic4 cloves (minced)
Heavy Cream1 cup
Butter2 tbsp
Parmesan Cheese1/2 cup (grated)
SaltTo taste
Black PepperTo taste

Details

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 2

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, melt butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.
  3. Lower the heat, pour in the heavy cream, and stir well. Let it simmer for a few minutes.
  4. Add the grated Parmesan cheese and stir until it melts into the sauce.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  6. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat evenly.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!

Spicy Chicken Tacos

Ingredients

IngredientQuantity
Chicken Breast2 pieces (cubed)
Taco Seasoning2 tbsp
Olive Oil2 tbsp
Tortillas4 small
Onions1 small (diced)
Tomatoes2 (diced)
Cilantro1/4 cup (chopped)
Hot SauceTo taste

Details

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 2

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the cubed chicken and cook until no longer pink.
  2. Sprinkle taco seasoning over the chicken and stir well to coat evenly.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  4. Assemble the tacos by adding chicken, onions, tomatoes, and cilantro to each tortilla.
  5. Drizzle with hot sauce for extra spice, and serve immediately.

Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

IngredientQuantity
Flour2 cups
Baking Soda1 tsp
Salt1/2 tsp
Butter1 cup (softened)
Sugar1/2 cup
Brown Sugar1/2 cup
Vanilla Extract1 tsp
Egg2 large
Chocolate Chips1 1/2 cups

Details

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 10-12 minutes
  • Serves: 24 cookies

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, cream the butter, sugar, and brown sugar until fluffy. Add vanilla extract and eggs, beating well.
  4. Gradually mix the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until combined.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving space between each cookie.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until golden brown. Let cool before serving.
